  <div class="pd-section-label">Overview</div>
  <div class="pd-body">
    In 2020, I co-founded <a href="https://criss-robotics.in/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">CRISS Robotics</a>, a student research technical team of 60+ members at BITS Pilani dedicated to designing and manufacturing an all-terrain 6-wheeled autonomous Mars rover. I developed the <span class="pd-highlight">high-level electrical architecture</span> and an <span class="pd-highlight">electronic differential-based drive control system</span> for the rover, capable of autonomous traversal, equipment servicing using a 6-DOF robotic manipulator, and life detection using a science module.
    <br/><br/>
    CRISS qualified for the <span class="pd-highlight">International Rover Challenge (IRC)</span> 2022 finals at Chennai, India, among 16 international teams, and subsequently won the <span class="pd-highlight">International Rover Design Challenge (IRDC)</span> in 2023.
